How to play

Cast. Wait. Hook.
Don't lose it.

1
Cast Your Line

Pick your depth — shore, open water, or the Abyss — and cast. Deeper = rarer fish, longer waits.

2
Wait for a Bite

Watch the bobber. When it dips and the alert fires, you've got a bite. Hit hook before the window closes.

3
Reel It In

Keep the fish marker inside the catch zone. Harder fish wander faster and drain your progress if you slip.

4
Sell & Upgrade

Sell your catch for coins, buy better rods and bait, unlock new worlds, and hunt for rare accessories.
